Introduction

This section describes how you can create a Finished Product from the Formula

To generate the Finished product, you must do the following:

  1. Step 1 - Populate the Formula Panels
  2. Step 2 - Create Specifications
  3. Step 3 - Create the Finished Product

Step 1 - Populate the formula panels

This section describes how to populate the formula panels

To populate panels, do the following:

  1. Open the search panel and define Formula from the drop-down list:
  2. Populate the following panels:
    • Allergens
    • Dietary
    • Religious
    • Physical
    • Specifications
    • Chemical

Step 2 - Create Specifications

Create two specifications in the Specifications panel

To create Specifications, do the following:

  1. Go to the Formula and open the Specifications panel
  2. Click the New Specification button
  3. In the New Specification dialog, select the Formula Specification
  4. Click the OK button.
  5. Click the New Specification button and select Label Specification from the drop-down list.

Step 3 - Create the Finished Product

Note:
To create the Finished product, first you need:
  • Populate the Formula panels
  • Create Specifications

To create the Finished product, do the following:

  1. Click the Actions button > select Create Finished Product from the drop-down list.
  2. In the Create Finished Product dialog:
    • Fill in the name of the product
    • Click the OK button.
The system creates the Finished product and opens its Navigator in the new tab.
